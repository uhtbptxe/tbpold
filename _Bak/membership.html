Membership Requirements


Tau Beta Pi - Texas Epsilon Chapter

University of Houston



To be eligable for membership in Tau Beta Pi a Engineering student must be in the top eighth of their class with Junior ranking or the top fifth of their class with Senior ranking. There are also certain class requirements that must be met depending upon the students specific major. These are as follows:
