Welcome to the home page of the Texas Epsilon Chapter of Tau Beta Pi at the University of Houston. You can find organization information and news here so please bookmark this page and check back frequently.

Tau Beta Pi Website Revisions The Tau Beta Pi website has gone under some changes.

  • Resume section has been updated
    If your resume is not listed and you want it to be, e-mail your resume to kle7@uh.edu in the following format:

    LastName_FirstName_Major.doc

    Also notify me if the link to your resume is not working.
  • Gallery section has been added
    Tau Beta Pi has long, active history at the University of Houston. However, we don't have any pictures to remember the past years! If you have pictures of Tau Beta Pi activities that you would like to share, please send them to me at the e-mail address listed above and I will post them up.
  • Calendar section has been added
    There is now a calendar that shows the dates of future events. Some dates will be subject to change and will be noted accordingly.

Fall '05 First Semester Meeting The first new initiate (as well as current members) meeting will be held on Tuesday September 20th.

DATE: Tuesday, September 20th.
TIME: 2:30 - 3:30 p.m.
LOCATION: D 102 (Auditorium next to TBP office)

This meeting will discuss what plans we have for the upcoming semester. Some events we would like to do this semester include:

  • Volunteering to help victims of Hurricane Katrina.
  • Engineering Futures sessions.
  • Volunteering at the BOS party.
  • Tau Beta Pi Fun Run.
  • T-shirt redesign contest.
