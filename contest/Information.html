Welcome to the web home of the first programming contest for high school students held at the University of Houston. This contest will be both a practice for the TCEA programming contest as well as giving high school students an opportunity to be exposed to Engineering and show them the resources available to them at the University of Houston. More information will be posted here as we get closer to the contest date. Please email tbp@egr.uh.edu to be added to the mailing list to obtain updates to the contest information as they become available.

Date: Saturday, January 29, 2005
Location: University of Houston
